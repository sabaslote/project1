What is Origami

Origami is the art of folding paper to construct models of your liking.Orgami originated from China and Akira Yoshizawa sparked the national interest in the art of Origami

  • Valley/ Mountain folding
  • Pleat fold
  • Outside Reverse Fold
  • Crimp Fold
  • Rabbit Ear
  • Inside Reverse Fold
  • Squash
  • Petal Fold
